About Us

TROPICAL RESOURCE GOVERNANCE FOR DEVELOPMENT is a non-partisan organisation founded in 2021. We invest in community-led governance, resource accountability, and justice in Northern Uganda.

Our Vision

Empowered people and accountable governance for a just and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To empower grassroots communities fostering sustainable natural resource and environmental exploitation, climate justice, social accountability, and livelihoods that enhance the quality of life and consider future generations.

Pages

Six integrated program areas designed to strengthen accountability, protect land rights, and build climate resilience.

Environment & Conservation

Supporting community land stewardship and wetland restoration.

Land Rights

Defending customary tenure and access to land for rural communities.

Climate Justice

Driving equitable climate adaptation with community voices.

Water & Wetlands

Strengthening water governance and sustainable wetland use.

Social Accountability

Supporting public oversight of service delivery and natural resource decisions.

Publishing evidence-based research for policy and community action.
